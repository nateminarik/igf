Services

At International Gourmet Foods, our goal is to provide our customers with the best service in the industry!

Here are a few services that set us apart from our competitors:
  • Late ordering deadlines
  • Low order minimums
  • Delivery five days a week by our all-temperature trucks to ensure product is kept at optimal integrity
  • Delivery by courier anywhere in North America
  • We employ a “Cheese Specialist”, “Dessert & Coffee Specialist”, and “Protein Specialist” to provide education for our sales force and our customers.
  • We provide specialized warehouse tours and personalized tastings! Spend a day with us at any of our three locations
  • Marketing service available. Ask your sales representative for more information
Who we service

International Gourmet Foods services a wide array of customers in the foodservice industry. We serve to restaurants, gourmet markets, clubs, caterers, bakeries, country clubs and hotels throughout our delivery region and via common carrier. We have dedicated ourselves to providing the highest quality food products, with the highest quality service level in the industry. This is why our customers are some of the finest chefs and people in the world and keep coming back to us.

Springfield, VA

Where it all began 27 years ago… home to our Corporate Office and largest distribution center. IGF Springfield services from Delaware to North Carolina and most places in between. Our office is conveniently located off of I-95 and minutes away from the beltway. We invite you to make an appointment for a private tour of our facilities and/or personalized tastings.

7520 Fullerton Rd Springfield, VA 22153, +1 703-569-4520

Charleston, South Carolina Division

Our Southeast distribution center is located in the heart of the low country and its ever growing culinary reputation, Charleston, South Carolina. This area continues to grow its place in the gastronomic scene in America and International Gourmet Foods is proud to serve this growth! Our staff has been selected right out of some of the best restaurants, bakery and food service businesses in the area and represents over 35 years of experience in the food business. Our delivery areas include all local Charleston areas, Myrtle Beach, Beaufort, Hilton Head, Columbia, Greenville, SC and Savannah, GA.

1105 Magnolia Rd, Charleston, SC 29407, +1 843-573-2220

Atlanta, Georgia Division

Our most southern distribution center is located in Atlanta, Georgia. It is one of our newest distribution centers within International Gourmet Foods. We are proud to continue to support the growth in the foodservice industry in the Atlanta area. Chefs from all over the area also have the opportunity to visit our warehouse for a tour and meet with our sales representatives. Our warehouse is located close to all major highways & Hartsfield-Jackson International Airport. Our delivery areas include all local Atlanta areas, Macon, Athens, Chattanooga, Tennessee, and now Birmingham Alabama.

5550 Fulton Industrial Blvd SW Atlanta, GA 30336, +1 404-349-6333
